Portfolio

  • Pixy Camera

    During my time at High School in FIRST Robotics, I programmed a system that used cameras to automatically play the game. It used a pair of PIXY cameras on the robot to locate the came objects, pick them up, target the goal, and automatically fire.

  • FANUC Hangman

    As mentioned in the about page, I worked with a FANUC robotic arm during my time in High School. I used this arm to play hangman against a human opponent, and it would flash lights at the player if they lost.

  • NKFL Navigation

    During my time at Rose Hulman, I began doing research with Dr. Yoder on navigating through NK fitness landscapes. An NK fitness landscape is a way to model the fitness of a given bitstring 'genome', where the chromosomes actually depend on each other and effect their fitness, rather than the more standard approach where each chromosome acts independently.

  • CNC Minimill

    A final experience I have had has been working with CNC minimills for automated manufacturing. I used these minimills to create precisely manufactured objects such as an aluminium Turner's Cube, a piece of rock with my initials engraved into it, and a small drawing of a formula 1 car.
